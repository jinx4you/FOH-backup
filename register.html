how to register !!!

  • Go to advancedpokertraining.com

    • For new players: Click the “Start Training” button to create your free account
    • For existing account holders, Click “Log In” and enter your username and password (if you are not automatically logged in)

  • From the Home Screen, click the “Private Live Games” tab

  • You are now at the Private Game Server Lobby. Click the Tournament tab to go to the list of available tournaments

  • On the tournament tab, scroll down to and select the tournament you are registering for (Thursday: Thursday Throwdown; Saturday: Saturday Showdown)

  • Click the “Register” button; you will be prompted for the password provided after the entry fee has been paid. Enter the password, click “OK”, and you are now registered!!
